Professional Profile of Ben Crittenden



My professional interests are IT Services, Software Development, and Data Analytics. My name is Ben Crittenden, and I have a passion for developing and deploying technology that enables people to be more productive in their work and to have more meaningful experiences in their lives. Python is a tool I particularly enjoy using, both for the fun of creating interesting projects as well as for its ability to increase productivity. Working with technology is something I find as fun as much as I find it important.

I have experience working in Information Technology and secondary education. My experience as a teacher has taught me organizational and communication skills that, coupled with my technical skills, enables me to be an asset for any organization with IT support needs.
